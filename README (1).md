# Career-Aligned Adventure: Internship Application Campaign

## Adventure Description

Over the 2024–2025 academic year, I conducted a large-scale internship job search targeting **Software Engineering (SWE)** and **Project Management (PM)** internship roles across the tech industry. I submitted approximately **350 applications**, tailored my resume for different role types, optimized my LinkedIn profile, and attended multiple WSU-hosted networking events to build professional connections and learn from recruiters and alumni firsthand.

## Career Goals

**Short-term:** Secure a summer 2025 internship in SWE or PM. While the primary application cycle has wound down, I am continuing to apply to roles that are still being posted.

**Long-term:** Graduate in Spring 2026 and land a full-time position in Project Management or Software Engineering. I am open to pursuing an internship into my senior year as an alternative path if a full-time offer does not materialize earlier.
